Death smiles upon us all. All a man can do is smile back.

You've got to let your ghosts

Pummel you to a pulp

Till there's solely

The mushy mess of blood,

       Flesh, and bone-dust.

Put it all in the blender

And you'll notice your teeth still rattle;

You only have the means to smile left!

What joy would that be.

                                    What bliss this

      Spiritual

                      Theft.
